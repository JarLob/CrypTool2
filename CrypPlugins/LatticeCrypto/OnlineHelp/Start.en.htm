Lattice-based cryptography

In this crypto tutorial you will learn about lattices and their use in cryptography especially cryptanalysis. This introduction is intended to provide the basics, to better understand the following chapters.
This is an early version. Please understand that many help texts are either missing or have not yet been translated.
