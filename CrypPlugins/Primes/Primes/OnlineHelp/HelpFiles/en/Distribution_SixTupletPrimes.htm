Prime sextuplet

A prime sextuplet is a group of 6 prime numbers with the distances 4, 2, 4, 2, and 4 between them. The middle four primes are therefore a prime quadruplet. The first prime number sextuplets are (7, 11, 13, 17, 19, 23), (97, 101, 103, 107, 109, 113) and (16057, 16061, 16063, 16067, 16069, 16073).
